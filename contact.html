Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue?

You can report issues through our Citizen App by taking a photo, adding a description, and pinning the location on the map.

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Response times vary based on issue severity, but most are addressed within 3-5 business days.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely.

Can I use CivicConnect anonymously?

You can report issues anonymously, but creating an account allows you to track your reports and receive updates.
